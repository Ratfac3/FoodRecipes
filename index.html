Semi-Sweet Choclate Chunk Cookie

Ingredients

▢ 1 1/2 cups butter, softened

▢ 3/4 cup granulated sugar

▢ 1 1/2 cup brown sugar

▢ 2 eggs

▢ 1 1/2 tbsp vanilla

▢ 4 1/4 cups all purpose flour

▢ 2 tsp baking soda

▢ 1 tsp baking powder

▢ 1 tsp salt

▢ 2 cups guittard milk chocolate chips

Instructions

1 Preheat oven to 375°.

2 Add the egg and vanilla. Mix until light in color and creamy.

3 Add in the dry ingredients (flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt), mix into the dough. Mix until completely combined.

4 Fold in the chocolate chips.

5 Portion out the dough into 1/2 cup portions. Roll into a ball, break the ball in half and squish the two halves together, leaving the rough edges up. Arrange 6 onto each cookie sheet. Add extra chocolate chips to the top if desired.

6 Bake for 12-15 minutes, until the cookies are turning golden brown.

7 Allow the cookies to cool on the pan for 20 minutes before serving or transferring to a wire rack.
